This privacy policy explains how we use the personal data collected from you when you use Shared Futures Space platform published by Animorph Ltd.

{% include "partials/vertical-spacer.html" with space="6" %}

What data do we collect?

We only collect data about web usage from logged in members. When you sign up for a Shared Futures account you provide us with:

{% include "partials/vertical-spacer.html" with space="3" %}
  • display name,
  • postcode (only the area part of it),
  • year of birth,
  • organisation you are associated with (optional).
{% include "partials/vertical-spacer.html" with space="4" %}

We record when you registered with us and allocate you a random unique user identifier. We also store when you log in to Shared Futures Space.

{% include "partials/vertical-spacer.html" with space="4" %}

As a registered user you have a profile view which displays your display name, avatar of choice, organisation (if you chose any) and the area you are associated with via postcode. We do not make your year of birth and postcode available to the public.

{% include "partials/vertical-spacer.html" with space="4" %}

When you are logged in, you can interact with other users through rivers (projects), resources and direct messaging. We record the following information about your participation in each of the three main modules:

Rivers

{% include "partials/vertical-spacer.html" with space="3" %}
  • chat contents,
  • poll votes.
{% include "partials/vertical-spacer.html" with space="3" %}

All information regarding rivers is visible to other users.

{% include "partials/vertical-spacer.html" with space="4" %}

Resources

{% include "partials/vertical-spacer.html" with space="3" %}
  • saved resources,
  • entered resources.
{% include "partials/vertical-spacer.html" with space="4" %}

Direct Messages

{% include "partials/vertical-spacer.html" with space="3" %}
  • direct messages you exchange with other users of the platform.

How do we collect your data?

We collect the data and metadata as you used the platform when you are logged in. Everything we collect is sent to our server located in the United Kingdom.
{% include "partials/vertical-spacer.html" with space="6" %}

How will we use your data?

We collect this data to better understand the needs of the Shared Futures stakeholders, and facilitate discussions about future developments in your community.

{% include "partials/vertical-spacer.html" with space="4" %}

We hold your data for the purpose of facilitating collaboration between the users (rivers with associated chats and polls) as well as knowledge-sharing (through resources).

{% include "partials/vertical-spacer.html" with space="4" %}

By submitting data, you consent to Animorph Ltd sharing it with our partner organisations, so that they may use it to make community improvements in their work based on the content.

Your data will not be used for any marketing purposes.
